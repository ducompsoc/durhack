What's DurHack?

DurHack is a 24-hour hackathon aiming to push the boundaries of technology to new limits.

We provide you with...

Mentors
Workshops
Mini-events
Prizes

Can I attend?

Yes! Registration is open to all current students from all Universities, over the age of 18. You can also register if you graduated in the last 12 months. Register here to secure your free place.

DurHack aims to be very welcoming and beginner-friendly, so there are a few things that don't matter:

It doesn't matter what degree you study.
It doesn't matter whether you've programmed before.
It doesn't matter if you haven't got a team.

All that matters is that you have a passion for technology and for learning something new!
