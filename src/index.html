What's DurHack?

DurHack is a 24-hour hackathon aiming to push the boundaries of technology to new limits, and we’re not letting COVID-19 get in the way of our mission!

We provide you with...

Mentors
Workshops
Mini-events
Prizes

Can I attend?

Yes! Anybody who is over 18 and a current registered student at Durham University (or has graduated in the last 12 months) can attend. Thanks to COVID-19, DurHack 2020 will be hack-from-home.

DurHack aims to be very welcoming and beginner-friendly, so there are a few things that don't matter:

It doesn't matter what degree you study.
It doesn't matter whether you've programmed before.
It doesn't matter if you haven't got a team.

All that matters is that you have a passion for technology and for learning something new!
